# Crea8ve Cool: Authority Page Blueprint (SOP)

This document defines the absolute standard for every service and detail page within the Crea8ve Cool ecosystem. Every page must adhere to this structure to ensure SEO dominance and high conversion.

---

## 1. Section Hierarchy & Logic
| Order | Section | Purpose | Mandatory Elements |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | **Hero Section** | Hook & Value Prop | H1 (Primary Keyword), 2 CTAs, Bento Visual Box |
| 2 | **The Pitch** | Detailed Offering | 5 Pitch Cards with high-quality copy and **Aggressive Linking** |
| 3 | **Strategic Sub-Nav** | Navigation | Sticky bar with anchors: Strategy, Performance, Tech, Pricing, FAQ |
| 4 | **Expert Pillars** | Authority Copy | 1,500+ words across 3 thematic sections (Strategy, Performance, Tech) |
| 5 | **Technical Glossary** | Education | Bootstrap Accordions explaining "Common Terms" (SEO, Speed, etc.) |
| 6 | **Ecosystem** | Siloing | Internal linking grid to other relevant service pages |

---

## 2. Linking Protocol (Aggressive)
- **Internal Velocity**: Link every major keyword (SEO, Marketing, CRM) to its respective service page.
- **External Authority**: Minimum 5 links per page to .edu, .gov, or high-authority tech sites (Google, Moz, HubSpot).
- **Frequency**: A meaningful link should appear every 50-100 words in the pillar sections.

---

## 3. Content Standards
- **Human-Aligned**: Avoid technical jargon without explanation. Use analogies to help customers understand value.
- **Plagiarism-Free**: Content must be manual-grade, non-AI-flagged, and specifically tailored to the Crea8ve Cool engineering stack.
- **Actionable**: Every section must end with a transition or a CTA that moves the user further down the funnel.

---

## 4. UI/UX Requirements
- **Functional CTAs**: Buttons like "View Features" or "See Specs" must jump to the corresponding `#id` on the page.
- **Responsive Mastery**: All bento grids and pitching cards must stack gracefully on mobile (1-column) and tablet (2-column).
- **Asset Integrity**: Only high-fidelity, WebP-optimized images or custom-generated infographics are permitted.
